Welcome to the Wildlife Licensing Online Portal

On this site you can apply online for specific wildlife licence types and manage your current licences and returns.

All correspondence regarding your applications, licences and returns and any other information or prompts will be sent to the email address that you have used to log into this system. Please remember to check this email regularly.

Apply for a new wildlife licence online

Applications

The “Applications” table below displays your pending applications. It also displays any draft applications that you have saved and not yet submitted.

Licences

As you begin using the online system, a summary of your current and past licences will be shown in the “Licences” table below.

Options in the “action” column for each licence allow you to apply to amend licences or have them reissued.

Returns

Depending on the types of licences you hold, you may be required to submit returns on a regular basis. These are accessible in the “Returns” table below. The table will contain any past returns submitted as well as returns due or overdue.
